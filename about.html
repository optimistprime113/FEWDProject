About the Podcast

Hello and welcome to the podcast Shrike Squad. We're a group of friends that got together to play a game of the Star Wars RPG and now we'd like to invite you to join us. The action will be thrilling (probably), the laughs will be plentiful (hopefully) and the heroes will be heroic (well maybe not all the time). Thanks for joining and as always May the Force be With You.

The Nesassio

The Nesassio is probably the most vital member of the crew. Acting as the transportation for Shrike Squad in most of their missions, the Nesassio is also deadly in combat. Able to withstand the stress of West's flying, Kura's gunnery, and more than a few encounters with the seedier element of the galaxy, with a little bit of tender care from the crew, the Nesassio can give any pilot in the Empire the dogfight of his life.
